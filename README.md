Golhars
Golhars is an online platform designed to showcase and sell paintings and crochet works. This project allows users to explore and purchase unique artwork, customize their purchases, and make secure payments. The website provides an intuitive and aesthetically pleasing interface for both casual visitors and art enthusiasts.It was .designed to simply put up my paintings commercially.

**Features**
Home Page: A welcoming page featuring the Golhars logo and links to different sections.
Paintings: Displays a collection of paintings available for purchase.
Crochet: Displays a collection of crochet works for sale.
Customize: Allows users to customize paintings based on size, color scheme, and orientation.
Cart: A shopping cart feature where users can view their selected products, add/remove items, and proceed to checkout.
FAQ: Frequently asked questions about Golhars and the services provided.
Contact: A contact page to connect with the Golhars team.
